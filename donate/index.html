! Support Us Financially

For students of Al-Hurriyah, providing mobile data plans can help them focus on studying, not on thinking about what necessities to sacrifice every week.

As a student organization, we are dependent on your support. We hope to provide Rp.100,000 each month to every student who receives a device from us, and we also need funding to fix damaged devices.

However small the amount, we ask you to donate on Kita Bisa today.

About your Device

We welcome any type of devices in all conditions; we will fix damaged ones. Mobile phones, taplets, and laptops are all in need.

Smartphones and tablets will be directly used by students, and computers will be used by another organization to benefit multiple students, under the 'Computer Care' project. We will document every progress in the 'Updates' page of this website.

Reset your Device

Both Apple and Android provide ways to reset your mobile devices completely. Factory reset is comprehensive and complete, as noted by experts. If you want extra security, delete your Icloud or Google account before reset. To ensure that devices are indeed used by students, we keep a record of their phone and asset numbers.

Drop off at JIS

Deliver your device(s) to the JIS High School Terogong Gate using any delivery method, such as Gojek and your personal car. JIS Security will kindly handle your donation. Please support us by covering delivery fee :)

Write down your full name and "Plugged-IN" on the delivery so that we can record you, unless you want to be anonymous.
