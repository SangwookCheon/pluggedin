Background and the Issue

For the last two years, before Covid-19, we introduced fun computer skills to young students in MI Al-Hurriyah, located only 700 meters away from Jakarta Intercultural School.

Today, many students in the school are struggling to access online education. Hafiz and Adieb are two of them.

Due to the long-term effects of the pandemic in Jakarta, they are forced to live and learn with uncertainty. With your support, we hope to help them.

The Power of Used Devices

Please support these students by donating an old device (smartphone, tablet, etc), or donating any amount of money to help buy sustainable data plans.

An old mobile device left in our drawer can mean so much for a family and a student in need. It can potentially enable months or even years of sustainable learning.

Imagine a student using your device every day to access online education, finally able to attend classes on time.

Wait!

If you are concerned about privacy issues with your old device, please read our comprehensive guide on the "Donate" page.

Your device will be set as the property of MI-Alhurriyah, allowing teachers to use your donations for any situation in the future, not just COVID-19.
